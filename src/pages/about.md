# About Planthor

Planthor is an open-source goal and objectives tracking application designed to support the journey of the **"Dragging to Dream" (D2D)** community.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a "keep moving forward" mentality by providing a platform where members can:

*   **Track Progress:** Securely manage and visualize personal running plans and athletic goals.
*   **Share Achievements:** Connect with a community of like-minded individuals to celebrate milestones.
*   **Collaborate:** Encourage contributions to the framework itself, creating a shared ecosystem of athletic and technical growth.

## Core Values

At the heart of Planthor are four foundational intentions:

1.  **Efficient Tech-Stacks:** We prioritize high-performance, scalable solutions.
2.  **Open-Source Framework:** We believe in transparency and collective improvement.
3.  **Encourage Contributions:** Every member is invited to help build the future of the platform.
4.  **Industrial Best-Practices:** We strike for practical, industrial-standard solutions in every aspect of development.

## The Community

Planthor is more than just an application; it is a tool for the **D2D community**. By providing practical solutions for goal tracking, we help athletes stay focused on their journey and connect with others who share their passion for continuous development.
